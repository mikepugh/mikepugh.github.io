FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about pricing, compatibility, and how PowerTread fits into your training workflow.

Can I try the app before I buy it?

Yes. PowerTread includes free sample courses and workouts, so you can evaluate the experience before purchasing. It is the fastest way to confirm your treadmill and connected devices are fully compatible with your setup.

Do I have to simulate a course to use the app?

No. PowerTread can run in Passive mode, where it records your session data and automatically adjusts power meter readings based on the treadmill's current incline. If you control incline manually on the treadmill, a single Bluetooth connection is enough. Two Bluetooth connections are only needed when another app, such as Zwift, is controlling incline while PowerTread is connected to read incline and record data.

Do I need to buy a subscription to PowerTread?

No. PowerTread Pro is a one-time, buy-once purchase that unlocks the full app for life, including future updates and new features. No subscriptions. No recurring fees. Ever.

What features are free to use?

PowerTread's sample courses, sample workouts, and Passive mode are available free of charge, with no usage limits.

What features require PowerTread Pro?

PowerTread Pro unlocks advanced import and sync capabilities, including adding your own GPX files to the course library, pulling courses from services like Strava, importing FIT structured workouts, and syncing planned workouts from platforms such as Intervals.icu.

Can I sync my completed activities to my training platform?

Yes. Completed activities can currently be synced to Strava and Intervals.icu, and additional platform integrations are in active development.
