Key Features

GPX Course Simulation

Load any GPX file and let PowerTread auto-adjust your treadmill's incline to perfectly simulate the course's terrain. Train for your next race, no matter where you live.

Dynamic Incline Control

Experience realistic elevation changes as PowerTread automatically controls your FTMS compatible treadmill's incline, mirroring every hill and descent.

Stryd Power Integration

Connect your Stryd Running Power Meter for on-screen power display, with values intelligently adjusted based on the treadmill's current incline for accurate training.

Passive Mode & Zwift Compatibility

Let other apps control incline while PowerTread records incline-adjusted running power, perfect for Stryd users on platforms like Zwift.

Optimized for KICKR Run

PowerTread delivers its best performance when paired with the Wahoo KICKR Run treadmill, offering seamless integration and an unparalleled training experience.

Available on iOS

Designed specifically for iOS, PowerTread provides an intuitive and powerful experience on your iPhone or iPad.
